Toum Recipe

Home


Description

Toum is a Lebanese garlic sauce made with garlic, oil, lemon juice, and salt. It’s creamy, fluffy, and strong in flavor—similar to aioli. Often served with grilled meats, shawarma, or pita.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1 pinch salt
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil, or as needed
  • 1 lemon, juiced

Steps:

  1. Crush garlic in a mortar and pestle with a generous pinch of salt until smooth.
    Mix in oil as needed, a teaspoon at a time, until mixture is emulsified.
  2. Stir in the lemon juice.
